About me

Let me introduce myself, my name is Paco Garcia, I'm a designer and illustrator, who combines work with teaching for 11 years. I live and I am from Barcelona and I was born in the district of Clot. I am graduate studies in illustration (Escola Massana) and a degree in Fine Arts, specializing in Design (University of Barcelona).
